New in 0.2.
Removed all built-in sound fallbacks.
Added 2 new brows buttons for choosing your own battery charge and discharge sounds.
Added 2 new combo boxes for setting the interval at which the battery percentage gets anounced when your computer gets plugged in, and when it's on power.
Renamed this addon to batteryUtils.
New in 0.1.
Added a toggleable checkbox that let's you play a sound to denote battery drops.
Added a key to check when the next battery alert will occur from the current battery percentage. You can press capslock plus alt plus shift to get this info.
